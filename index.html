How to Achieve the Perfect Glowy, Natural “No-Makeup” Makeup Look with Glossier

#1 Blog for Beauty Hacks

Why Glossier?

In a world where beauty trends come and go, there’s one look that never goes out of style: the effortless, natural, “no-makeup” makeup look. It's all about enhancing your natural features and achieving that coveted dewy glow. Glossier is known for believing in embracing your true beauty with minimalist products that let your skin shine through. Here’s how you can create a flawless, glowy, no-makeup makeup look using our favorite Glossier essentials.

Step 1: Prep Your Canvas with Priming Moisturizer

The key to a glowy, natural look is starting with well-hydrated skin. Begin by applying Glossier's Priming Moisturizer to your face. This lightweight, buildable cream provides the perfect base by smoothing and plumping your skin, ensuring your makeup glides on effortlessly. The best part? It hydrates without feeling heavy, keeping your skin looking fresh and dewy all day long.

Step 2: Perfect Your Complexion with Skin Tint

Forget heavy foundations—our Perfecting Skin Tint is all you need for a natural, even complexion. This breathable, sheer coverage formula evens out your skin tone while letting your natural beauty shine through. Apply a few drops to your fingertips and blend it outwards from the center of your face for a soft, diffused effect. It’s your skin, but better.

Step 3: Conceal with Stretch Concealer

For those little imperfections that need a bit more coverage, reach for the Stretch Concealer. Its flexible, buildable formula moves with your skin rather than sitting on top of it, providing a natural finish. Dab it under your eyes, around your nose, or on any blemishes, and blend with your fingertips. This ensures your complexion looks even and radiant without appearing cakey.

Step 4: Add a Healthy Flush with Cloud Paint

No natural look is complete without a touch of color on the cheeks. Glossier's Cloud Paint in shades like “Beam” or “Dusk” gives you that perfect, subtle flush. Just a tiny dot is enough—tap it onto the apples of your cheeks and blend it upwards toward your temples for a fresh, youthful glow. The gel-cream formula melts into your skin, making it look like you’re naturally blushing.

Step 5: Highlight Your Features with Haloscope

Achieving a dewy glow is all about the right highlighter. Glossier’s Haloscope is your go-to for that lit-from-within radiance. Apply it to the high points of your face—cheekbones, brow bones, and the bridge of your nose. The hydrating formula is infused with coconut and castor seed oils, giving your skin a dewy finish without any sparkle, just pure, natural-looking sheen.

Step 6: Define Your Brows with Boy Brow

Natural beauty starts with well-groomed brows. Use Boy Brow to fluff and shape your brows into place, adding just a hint of color and hold. The creamy wax formula thickens and conditions your brows without feeling stiff, giving them a fuller, yet still natural appearance.

Step 7: Enhance Your Lashes with Lash Slick

Skip the heavy falsies and opt for Glossier’s Lash Slick to enhance your natural lashes. This film-form mascara lengthens and defines each lash without clumping, creating a fluttery, natural look. It's water-resistant, so you’ll maintain that fresh, wide-eyed appearance all day.

Step 8: Finish with a Pop of Shine from Balm Dotcom

Complete your look with a swipe of Balm Dotcom on your lips. Choose from a variety of subtle tints, like “Rose” or “Cherry,” for a hint of color. This hydrating balm not only nourishes your lips but also gives them a soft, glossy finish that ties the whole look together.
